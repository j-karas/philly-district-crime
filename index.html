In this project, we collected data on the crime incidents in Philadelphia in the year 2023 thus far. Each crime is divided into one of the 22 police districts and is defined by their type (ie. theft, robbery firearm, burglary residential). The purpose of this website is to allow the user to pick two districts from dropdown menus and compare the crime frequencies between the two. In a double bar graph, the two districts' crime types are compared based on frequency. Our goal is to provide users accurate information about crime in Philadelphia; our city has an unfortunate reputation of being very dangerous, but is it really dangerous as everyone says it is? With the information provided, the user will be well-informed enough to create their opinion and answer the question themselves.
